Autobiography

Bad half

I began studying in 2018 September. I enrolled in the University of Calgary in the Fall semester in natural sciences then I transferred into computer science within the first year or the second year. I was not a diligent student, calculus and physics were easy because I had already done those in highschool but everything else was hard and boring because I didn't really care about it and I did not study. German language classes were interesting. I took photography, photo and video editing, and 3D modelling as options.


Good half

My family moved to Windsor, Ontario across the Detroit River from Detroit, at that time Jesus Christ changed me and saved me, and I started to know God. After I started studying at the University of Windsor (because I had transferred my bachelor degree studies) I found great interest in working hard for the sake of respecting the gifts God gave me and obeying God and enjoying his wisdom in my life. Because of that I became more interested in my studies, I find logic and proofs to be the most interesting subject, I am not very creative, but I like the more math oriented parts of computer science, although I think they are harder. Databases are interesting to me as well.
